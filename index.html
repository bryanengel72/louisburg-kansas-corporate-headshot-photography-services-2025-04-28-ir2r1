Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my corporate headshot?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that reflects your industry. Avoid busy patterns and bright colors. Business professional attire typically works best - suits, blazers, or conservative dresses. We'll provide a detailed preparation guide upon booking.

How long does a headshot session take?

Standard sessions typically last 30-45 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses and outfits. We ensure a relaxed atmosphere to capture your best expressions and provide guidance throughout the session.

When will I receive my photos?

You'll receive a gallery of proofs within 48 hours of your session. After selecting your preferred images, final retouched photos are delivered within 3-5 business days in both high-resolution and web-optimized formats.

Do you offer group rates for businesses?

Yes, we offer special packages for corporate teams and can accommodate on-site sessions for groups of 5 or more. Contact us for custom pricing and scheduling options for your team.

What's included in the final delivery?

You'll receive professionally retouched images in both high-resolution and web-optimized formats, perfect for print and digital use. Package includes commercial usage rights and multiple file formats for various applications.
